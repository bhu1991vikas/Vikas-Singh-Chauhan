About

I am a physicist working in the field of quantum computation and quantum simulation using ultracold atomic platforms. My research focuses on collective light-matter interactions such as superradiance and subradiance, using ⁸⁷Rb atoms excited via Rapid Adiabatic Passage techniques. I am particularly interested in exploring coherent control and many-body dynamics in cold atom systems.

Research Interests

  • Out-of-equilibrium dynamics and collective quantum behavior
